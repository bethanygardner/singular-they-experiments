This study involves reading PSA (public service announcement) texts on various topics, reading descriptions of different people, and then answering questions about what you have read.

Some of the questions ask you to record yourself speaking out loud. To do this, you need to have a computer with a microphone, be in a quiet place, and be comfortable speaking English.

After you read the consent form, your browser will ask for permission to use your microphone, and there will be a test trial to make sure it is working.
